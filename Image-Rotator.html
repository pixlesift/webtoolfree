Key Features

  • Completely Private: Your images are processed directly in your browser. They never leave your computer.
  • No Uploads: Since nothing is sent to a server, the process is instant and uses zero bandwidth.
  • Works Offline: Once the page is loaded, you can disconnect from the internet and the tool will still work perfectly.
  • Easy to Use: A clean, simple interface gets the job done without any complicated settings.
  • Multiple Formats: Supports common image formats like PNG, JPEG, GIF, and BMP.

How to Use

  1. Click the "Select an Image" button and choose a file from your computer.
  2. Your image will appear in the box above. Use the control buttons (Rotate Left/Right, Flip) to adjust it.
  3. When you are happy with the result, click the "Download Image" button to save the new image to your computer.

Is It Safe? Your Privacy is Guaranteed.

Absolutely. This tool is 100% safe and private.

Unlike many online image editors, this page uses client-side JavaScript to perform all operations. This means all the code runs entirely on your computer, inside your web browser.

When you select an image, it is loaded directly from your device into the browser's memory. It is never transmitted, uploaded, or sent over the internet to any server. The moment you close this browser tab, the image is gone. This makes the tool as secure as any offline desktop software.
